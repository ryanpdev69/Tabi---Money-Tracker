# Tabi - Money Tracker

## 🇵🇭 Your Simple & Private Money Tracker

Tabi is a simple offline money tracker made for Filipinos who want an easier way to manage their daily finances.  
Track your income, expenses, bills, and multiple wallets — all in one clean and private app.

No accounts. No ads. No internet required.

Your money stays yours.

---

# ✨ Why Choose Tabi?

Managing money today can be stressful, especially when using different wallets and banks like GCash, Maya, GoTyme, or traditional bank accounts.

Tabi helps make money tracking simple by giving you:

- 💳 A single place to track all your wallets and accounts
- 🔒 Complete privacy with offline storage
- ⚡ A fast and easy-to-use experience
- 📱 A clean interface without clutter or distractions

Whether you're tracking daily expenses, monthly bills, or your remaining balance, Tabi helps you stay organized without the complexity of traditional budgeting apps.
